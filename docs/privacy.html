Privacy Policy

HyperClip — Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

HyperClip accesses your YouTube account solely to retrieve your subscription list and detect new video uploads. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to third parties.

2. OAuth Access

We use Google OAuth 2.0 to authenticate with YouTube. The only scopes requested are:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/youtube.readonly — read your subscriptions and uploaded videos

We do not request write access to your YouTube account.

3. Local Storage

All OAuth tokens and application data are stored locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

4. Cookies

This application does not use web cookies.

5. Data Retention

Downloaded videos are stored locally on your device. You may delete them at any time.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns: support@hyperclip-app.github.io
